About Us

Droplet is a young company based in Guadalajara, Mexico that aims to provide
an efficient way to monitor and have total control of the irrigation system for crop fields
as an effort to push the tecnification of Mexican agricultural sector.

The approach is to have an affordable and easy-to-use network of small, but powerful devices that
provide mexican farmers with accurate and real time metrics of their crops

Intelligent and practical monitoring of your crops

With a local network of Droplets on your crop fields, awareness of the health of your crops becomes a trivial task now!

With a single hub of Droplets you can monitor up to half hectare of crops fields and get essential information remotely.

  • Accurate nutrient values.
  • Accurate Weather conditions inside / outside your greenhouse.
  • Remote control of your irrigation system.

Droplet lets you build your own network of monitoring system at your own peace with sofisticated technology
